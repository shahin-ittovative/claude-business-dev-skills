# Porter's Five Forces

A tool to analyze the competitive pressure of the market on a similar product or feature. It measures the level of risk or threat for the product.

## Scoring rule

Each force is rated on a threat scale from 1 to 3: 1 = low risk / threat, 2 = medium, 3 = high risk / threat. The total score is the average of the five force ratings.
