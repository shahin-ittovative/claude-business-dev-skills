# Ansoff Matrix

Not a numerical calculation tool but a decision matrix to choose the growth strategy of the product. There are four classifications; decide which one the product belongs to.

## The four strategies

- Market Penetration (existing market, existing product): grow by increasing sales of existing products to current markets.
- Market Development (new market, existing product): grow by entering new geographic or demographic markets with existing products.
- Product Development (existing market, new product): grow by adding new features, versions, or services for current users.
- Diversification (new market, new product): grow by launching completely new products for new markets.

Rate each strategy across the criteria below (for example, on a 1 to 5 scale or High / Medium / Low). The final score is the decision maker: it indicates the growth strategy the product should follow. The rating scheme is not fixed; you may add more ratings or criteria.
