# IFAS (Internal Factors Analysis Summary)

Part of the SWOT analysis. IFAS focuses on identifying the internal strengths and weaknesses of the resources affecting the product. Each factor is captured with four responses (type, weight, rating, weighted score).

## Scoring rules

- Type: Strength (S) or Weakness (W).
- Weight: importance of the factor, from 0 to 1. The sum of all factor weights must equal 1.
- Rating: internal performance on this factor, from 1 (poor / weak) to 5 (strong).
- Weighted score = Weight x Rating, computed per factor.
- Total score = sum of all weighted scores.
