# SWOT Analysis

A strategic planning tool used to identify and assess the internal and external factors that impact a business, product, or project. It highlights what works, what does not, and what to watch for. Strengths and weaknesses are internal (see IFAS); opportunities and threats are external (see EFAS).

| Category | What to capture | Answer |
|---|---|---|
| Strengths | What you do well (internal, positive). |  |
| Weaknesses | What you lack or need to improve (internal, negative). |  |
| Opportunities | External trends or chances to grow (external, positive). |  |
| Threats | External risks or challenges (external, negative). |  |
