# PESTLE Analysis

A scan of external macro factors. Use when entering a new market or geography, launching a new product, doing strategic planning, managing risk, or evaluating M&A decisions.

| Factor | What to assess | Answer |
|---|---|---|
| Political | Government policies, regulations, political stability, trade restrictions. |  |
| Economic | Inflation, exchange rates, interest rates, economic growth, labor costs. |  |
| Social | Cultural trends, demographics, lifestyle changes, education, social values. |  |
| Technological | Innovation, automation, R&D activity, technology change, digital trends. |  |
| Legal | Employment laws, health and safety regulations, consumer laws, industry rules. |  |
| Environmental | Climate change, sustainability, environmental regulations, eco-trends. |  |
